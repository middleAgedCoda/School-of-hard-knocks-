Q: "How did you actually get here?"

From 25K
to 20 million.

2021
Three friends launch a business advice channel
James, Jack, and Joshua start posting. 25,000 followers. Kids giving business advice. Nobody cares. The first version fails and they know it within months.
2021
The Pivot — go find the experts
"We figured that most people don't give a shit about 20-year-old kids giving business advice. We went to find those industry experts that have 20, 30, 40, 50 years of experience." They take the classroom to the streets.
2022
Mark Cuban — the pivotal interview
Cold-approached Mark Cuban after a South by Southwest event. He stops. He talks. The video performs. "If Mark Cuban is stopping to do an interview with us, why can't we get everybody else?" Confidence: confirmed. The race begins.
2023
Scale — posting relentlessly · travelling everywhere
James publishes up to 3 times a day in the early phase. The team is relentlessly travelling to get interviews. Every day. Every city. Every sidewalk. The format finds its rhythm. TikTok, Instagram, YouTube all growing simultaneously.
2024
First $100K month · School of Mentors launches
June 2024: School of Mentors community launched on Skool. Now 6,000+ members. First $100K month arrives. 3 years of showing up before the first six-figure month. "Consistency is everything." The content agency launches. The consulting practice launches.
2025
$500K+ every month · Tom Cruise · Fox & Friends · 20M total
Every single month in 2025 has exceeded $500,000 in revenue. Tom Cruise requests an interview. All three founders appear on Fox & Friends October 2025. Hard Knocks Women and Hard Knocks Business sub-channels launch. VUE Magazine. Roe Magazine. Target: 9-figure media ecosystem.
4B+

The formula
that never
changes.

The psychological hook is permanent: authority proximity + curiosity gap + fast-value delivery + relatability. A random person on a street. A millionaire stops. 60 seconds. Life advice that most people spend $200,000 on a degree not to receive. The format is a machine. It doesn't stop working.

The Expansion
